Description
The ultimate neon snake io battle arena! Slither, eat, grow, and dominate the leaderboard in this addictive snake vs snake combat game.

Grow from a tiny worm into a massive serpent. Outsmart enemies, collect power-ups, and crush the King for epic rewards. Classic snake io gameplay with modern twists and 52 unique skins to unlock.

◆ WHY PLAYERS LOVE IT

• Addictive fast-paced snake battles
• Smooth one-thumb touch controls
• Offline play — no wifi needed
• 52 collectible snake skins with neon, rainbow, striped & gradient styles
• Dynamic zones that change as you grow
• Daily rewards + coin economy
• Pause, settings, tutorial — polished experience

◆ GAME MODES & FEATURES

• Battle Arena: Compete against 12 intelligent AI snakes
• King Boss Fight: Take down the giant King snake for 500 bonus points
• Combo System: Chain food pickups for 2x and 3x score multipliers
• Power-Ups: Speed, Shield, Magnet, and Ghost mode
• Local Leaderboard: Track your top 10 best scores
• Daily Login Bonus: Come back every day for free coins + streak rewards

◆ HOW TO PLAY

• Touch and drag to steer your snake
• Eat glowing food orbs to grow longer
• Hit the BOOST button for a speed burst
• Crash enemies into your body to eliminate them
• Bigger snake wins head-to-head collisions
• Avoid walls and opponent bodies!

◆ PRO TIPS

• Use the magnet power-up to collect distant food
• Shield protects from one fatal hit
• Ghost mode lets you phase through enemies
• Trap smaller snakes in tight circles
• Save boost fuel for escape and attack moments
• Longer snakes get bigger rewards from kills
