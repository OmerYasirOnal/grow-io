◆ HOW TO PLAY

• Touch and drag to steer your snake
• Eat glowing food orbs to grow longer
• Hit the BOOST button for a speed burst
• Crash enemies into your body to eliminate them
• Bigger snake wins head-to-head collisions
• Avoid walls and opponent bodies!

◆ PRO TIPS

• Use the magnet power-up to collect distant food
• Shield protects from one fatal hit
• Ghost mode lets you phase through enemies
• Trap smaller snakes in tight circles
• Save boost fuel for escape and attack moments
• Longer snakes get bigger rewards from kills
