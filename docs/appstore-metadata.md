## Description
Grow your snake, dominate the arena! Start small, eat food to grow, and eliminate other players by cutting across their path. How long can you survive?

FEATURES:
- Smooth joystick controls for precise movement
- Massive 3000x3000 battle arena with up to 12 players
- 4 powerful power-ups: Speed Boost, Shield, Magnet, Ghost
- 50+ unique skins to unlock
- Minimap to track your enemies
- Dynamic difficulty that scales with your size
- Boss battles against the King Snake every 2000 points
- 5 visual themes that change as you grow
- Neon dark aesthetic with glowing effects
- Watch ads to revive and continue your run

TIPS:
- Collect food near the center for fast early growth
- Use boost to cut off smaller snakes
- Grab the Shield power-up before hunting bigger snakes
- Stay away from the boundary - it's deadly!

Can you become the biggest snake in the arena?
